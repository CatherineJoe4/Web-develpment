ADMIRATION FOR THE LEHENGAS

Lehenga history begins with the Mughal era, which is when this garment was first worn by women. It flourished during that period, quickly becoming the most commonly worn garment not just amongst the rich, upper-class women and royals, but also amongst the masses.

Ghagra choli, (also known as lehenga choli and locally as chaniya choli), is a type of ethnic clothing for women from Indian Subcontinent, notably in the Indian states of Rajasthan, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Haryana, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Jammu and Kashmir
