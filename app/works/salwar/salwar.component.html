THE ELEGANCE OF SUITS

It's the most casual, ready-to-go and always-trendy apparel you'll ever have. But do you know there are over 20 styles of a salwar suit alone.
The history of salwar kameez as a garment is quite rich.

The salwar is also known as churidar and is paired with the Kameez. It is worn by women across India and is a famous traditional dress. Kameez is varied from being ankle-length Anarkali suits to short Kurtis.
